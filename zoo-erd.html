Design Notes

Inheritance strategy: animal is the parent entity, while predator, penguin, and fish are subtype tables linked through animal_id.

Normalization: species removes hardcoded species metadata from the Java code, and fish_color supports multi-valued colors cleanly.

Operational tracking: feeding_event and death_record make the current in-memory behavior persistable and reportable.
